### What is our goal with Mosaic?
    Improve Alpha capture. 
        We generate alpha by staying invested in attractive secular trends (we organize them by thematic buckets). This requires conviction, and we gain conviction by continuously weighing evidence of the secular trend  
        against countervailing or disruptive factors. 
        For example, is AI really driving an acceleration in drug discovery? Are scaling laws going to continue? There are much smarter people working on these issues, and we have the humility to say we don't know and
        we can't predict the future any better than the next investor. Instead we must be honest with ourselves as confirmatory or contradictory evidence emerge. Finding evidence for or against a secular trend tends
        to be relatively straight forward: corporations announce major milestones, elections bring in policy change, the world changes and evolves. The majority of our efforts are spent analyzing the disruptive 
        factors. 
        When we have a portfolio of diversified ideas, our willingness to sit through volatility is what ultimately delivers alpha. 

### How Mosaic achieves our goal?
    We can better tie portfolio performance to factors driving our thematic buckets. Understand why buckets are working or not working. And then from there, we can understand the risk of potential change. 
    If we can have a better idea of what's driving our portfolio, we have a better idea if we are adequately diversified. We can see if we are taking risk in areas we don't want to be involved in and/or at levels
    that we don't want. We can objectively assess which ideas we want hold the most conviction in, and we can discard the rest.
