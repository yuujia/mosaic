# Thesis

## Thematic Color
- Prosigna: breast cancer during therapy phase, to assess likelihood of distant recurrence. Helps decide if patients still need chemotherapy or can switch to hormone therapy
- Decipher: prostate and bladder cancer, tumor prognostic test
- Afirma: thyroid cancer, therapy selection
- TrueMRD: MRD for bladder cancer. $4000 ASP pricing.
- Percepta: nasal swab for lung cancer screening for former smokers with nodules
- Threat from Digital Pathology: Decipher currently more accurate vs multimodal AI scores. AI creates a lot of false negatives. Decipher is backed by 200,000 transcriptomes.
