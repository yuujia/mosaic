# CANCER Bucket Thesis

Bucket Symbol: .CANCER  
Bucket Folder: CANCER  
Side: Long  
Last Updated: 2026-03-03  

---

## Core Thesis

Advances in genomic sequencing and biomarker discovery expand molecular testing for cancer diagnosis, therapy selection and monitoring.  

---

## Structural Drivers (Required Conditions)

These must remain true for the thesis to work:

- Molecular biomarker discovery continues to expand clinically actionable testing menus.
- Molecular testing costs continue to decline, broadening accessibility and adoption.
- Guideline inclusion and reimbursement coverage convert new testing into recurring clinical volume.
-  

---

## Company-Level Evidence

Evidence that structural drivers of the investment thesis are playing out at the company level.

### On Track Definition

The investment thesis is playing out at the company level when:

- Positive clinical trials, regulatory approvals, and reimbursement decisions.
- Revenue and volume acceleration driven by molecular menu expansion.
- Margin expansion demonstrating ROI on menu expansion.

### Off Track Definition

The investment thesis is deteriorating at the company level when:

- Revenue deceleration from slower testing volumes or pricing pressure.
- Margin contraction reflecting poor ROI on menu expansion.
- Negative reimbursement or regulatory announcements.

---

## Structural Failure Modes

Macro, industry, or structural forces that can overwhelm the thesis:

- Changes to government and commercial payor policies.
- Competition crowding out a market defined by a relatively fixed patient population
