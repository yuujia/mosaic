# Thesis

## Thematic Color
- Guardant360 is therapy selection for advanced solid tumors (stage 3 and 4). Tumor agnostic but often for non-small cell lung cancer, CRC, prostate
- Guardant Reveal: MRD product that is now expanding into therapy monitoring. 50% of volumes now is for CRC
- Guardant Shield: CRC Screening

## Diligence Questions
- AstraZeneca Serena-6 trial: why is G360 Liquid being used for monitoring instead of Reveal? (for breast cancer ESR1 mutation)
- How quickly do volumes respond when ACS guidelines change? Is it still dependent on sales channel?
- What is pricing upside potential for G360 and Reveal?

### Quarterly KPI Assessment
- Revenues continued accelerating and margins continued expanding

### Key KPI Drivers
- Screening revenue contribution: $24M or ~10% of sales 
- Oncology revenues decelerated: 30% yoy. Reveal growing ~100% and G360 growing ~25%
- Oncology price mix lower: Reveal at ~$700 while G360 at ~$3000

### Forward Trajectory
- Revenue deceleration: FY26 guidance of $1.25B - $1.28B (28.5% yoy v 39.4% yoy last year) 
- Oncology segment deceleration: FY26 guidance of 26% revenue growth and 30% volume growth
- Growing Screening segment contribution: FY26 guidance of $162M-$174M (110% yoy), 210k-225k tests (150% yoy), implies ASPs going from $850 to $775  
- Margin expansion: FY26 guidance free cash flow burn of $185M - $195M (vs $233M last Year)

### Upside Drivers
- Screening: ACS guideline inclusion, Quest partnership, sales productivity in PCP channel
- Oncology: G360 Liquid submitted for FDA approval, which would receive ADLT pricing and drive volumes
- Oncology: Reveal submitted to MolDX for chemomonitoring 

### Risk Factors (Macro, Execution, Structural)
- Execution: Screening ASP declining given higher commercial mix vs MA. 
- Execution: G360 Liquid awaiting FDA approval
- Execution: Reveal awaiting MolDX inclusion

### Bucket Implications
- Confirms secular thesis: molecular testing in oncology a rapidly growing market
- Risk from competition: a lot of innovation pursuing MRD, where its ultimately a fixed patient population
- Reimbursement risk: payor reimbursement decisions (MolDX, commercial) dictate size of addressable market
