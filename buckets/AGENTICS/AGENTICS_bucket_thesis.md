# AGENTICS Bucket Thesis

Bucket Symbol: .AGENTICS  
Bucket Folder: AGENTICS  
Side: Long / Short  
Last Updated: 2026-03-10  

---

## Core Thesis

AI accelerates competition in the software industry, and companies that do not operate a System of Record are at particular risk.  

---

## Structural Drivers (Required Conditions)

These must remain true for the thesis to work:

- Competition from AI startups, LLM services, and agentic offerings by larger software companies.

---

## Company-Level Evidence

Evidence that structural drivers of the investment thesis are playing out at the company level.

### On Track Definition

The investment thesis is playing out at the company level when:

- Revenue deceleration and margin contraction
- Competitor announcements
- 

### Off Track Definition

The investment thesis is deteriorating at the company level when:

- Revenue acceleration and margin expansion 
- Company's own agentic offerings gain traction
-
