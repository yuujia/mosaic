### Quarterly KPI Assessment
- Revenues accelerate but EBITDA margins shrink and capex accelerates.

### Key KPI Drivers
- Fuel Services Volumes: Accelerated to 6% yoy (3.8M lbs) vs -3% yoy last quarter. New contracts rolling through

### Forward Trajectory
- Declining Uranium production: Guiding 19.5-21.5M lbs in FY26, compared to 21M lbs in FY25
- Declining Fuel Services volume: Guiding to 13M - 14M kgU in FY26, compared to 14M kgU in FY25
- Declining Westinghouse EBITDA: Guiding to $370M - $430M in FY26, compared to $627M in FY26 (but FY25 included a royalty payment in Czechia).

### Upside Drivers
