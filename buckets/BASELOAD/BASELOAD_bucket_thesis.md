# BASELOAD Bucket Thesis

Bucket Symbol: .BASELOAD  
Bucket Folder: BASELOAD  
Side: Long / Short  
Last Updated: 2026-04-15  

---

## Core Thesis

Private and public sector investments drive long-term demand for new baseload power. Companies that enable natural gas and nuclear power will benefit. 

---

## Structural Drivers (Required Conditions)

These must remain true for the thesis to work:

- Project activity going FID: drives a growing backlog for long lead-time suppliers 
- Favorable government policies for gas and nuclear: pipeline build and reactor builds have been historical roadblocks 

---

## Company-Level Evidence

Evidence that structural drivers of the investment thesis are playing out at the company level.

### On Track Definition

The investment thesis is playing out at the company level when:

- Accelerating orders and revenues
- Accelerating capex
- Project announcements
- 

### Off Track Definition

The investment thesis is deteriorating at the company level when:

- Decelerating orders and revenues
- Decelerating capex
- Project delays
- 

---

## Structural Failure Modes

Macro, industry, or structural forces that can overwhelm the thesis:

- Industry: delays in nuclear projects 
- Structural: election changes policy stance towards energy infrastructure and AI 
- Macro: slowdown in investment activity, notably for datacenters and manufacturing
