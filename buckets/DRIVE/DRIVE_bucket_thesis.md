# DRIVE Bucket Thesis

Bucket Symbol: .DRIVE  
Bucket Folder: DRIVE  
Side: Long / Short  
Last Updated: 2026-03-03  

---

## Core Thesis

Migration trends out of urban areas imply secular growth in miles driven, benefiting retail-facing businesses that service the American car parc.

---

## Structural Drivers (Required Conditions)

These must remain true for the thesis to work:

- Combination of growing miles driven and aging vehicle population.
- Migration trends out of urban areas continue.
- Job growth and investment activity (infrastructure, energy, datacenters, manufacturing)

---

## Company-Level Evidence

Evidence that structural drivers of the investment thesis are playing out at the company level.

### On Track Definition

The investment thesis is playing out at the company level when:

- Accelerating sales and/or same store sales
- Margin expansion
- 

### Off Track Definition

The investment thesis is deteriorating at the company level when:

- Decelerating sales and/or same store sales
- Margin contraction
- 

---

## Structural Failure Modes

Macro, industry, or structural forces that can overwhelm the thesis:

- Structural: Meaningful growth of electric vehicles within US car parc
- Macro: Slowdown in US economy
