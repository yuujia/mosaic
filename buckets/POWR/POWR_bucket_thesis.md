# POWR Bucket Thesis

Bucket Symbol: .POWR  
Bucket Folder: POWR  
Side: Long / Short  
Last Updated: 2026-03-03  

---

## Core Thesis

Higher domestic fixed investments accelerate electricity demand, allowing regulated utilities to grow their rate base and recover attractive ROE.

---

## Structural Drivers (Required Conditions)

These must remain true for the thesis to work:

- Electricity demand growth supported by investments in data centers, manufacturing, general electrification.
- State regulators allow favorable rate cases on to higher allowed ROE and recovery on works in progress via bill increases or large customer tariffs
- Utilities are able to maintain investment grade balance sheets

---

## Company-Level Evidence

Evidence that structural drivers of the investment thesis are playing out at the company level.

### On Track Definition

The investment thesis is playing out at the company level when:

- New projects are announced and converted to construction and energy service agreements
- Favorable regulatory outcomes related to rate base and allowed ROE
- Backlog is converted into capex plans
- 

### Off Track Definition

The investment thesis is deteriorating at the company level when:

- FFO-Debt deteriorates due to bad execution
- Unfavorable outcomes with regulators
- Slowing deal announcements

---

## Structural Failure Modes

Macro, industry, or structural forces that can overwhelm the thesis:

- Slowdown in data center and electrification capex
- Political pushback pressures regulators
- Company misexecution leads to dilutive equity raises
