NEO

Recent quarter trend
- Q4 2025 revenue growth: +10.5%
- Q4 2025 test-volume growth: +10.7%
- Q4 2025 revenue per test: about $488, up +4.9%
- Quarter read: revenue decelerated, gross margin still contracted YoY, but G&A savings helped offset that
- Operational read: shifting capacity toward higher-value categories, so slower growth but improving mix

Forward quarter trend
- Q1 2026 revenue growth: +9.9%
- Q2 2026 revenue growth: +8.4%
- Q3 2026 revenue growth: +9.6%
- Q4 2026 revenue growth: +10.8%
- Q1 2026 test-volume growth: +6.0%
- Q2 2026 test-volume growth: +3.0%
- Q3 2026 test-volume growth: +5.5%
- Q4 2026 test-volume growth: +6.0%
- Q1 2026 revenue per test growth: +6.0%
- Q2 2026 revenue per test growth: +6.5%
- Q3 2026 revenue per test growth: +5.0%
- Q4 2026 revenue per test growth: +5.5%
- Forward read: estimates assume low-to-mid single-digit volume growth, with pricing and mix doing a lot of the work

Guidance
- FY26 revenue: $793M-$801M, about +9.7%
- Volume growth: low-to-mid single digits
- Pricing: upper single digits
- Revenue cadence: about 10% in Q1, 8-9% in Q2, 9-10% in Q3, >10% in Q4
- RaDaR ST contribution: mid-single-digit millions
- EBITDA: $55M-$57M, up 27-31%

Upside drivers
- RaDaR ST gets two additional indications through MolDX
- PanTracer LBx contribution improves after coverage and 2H26 ramp
- PanTracer Tissue continues strong growth, helped by PanTracer Pro
- Higher-value mix lifts revenue per test more than expected

Risks
- Pharma business declines before next-gen RaDaR fills the gap
- Rationalizing older tests costs more volume than expected
- Broad genomic profiling remains competitive and mature
- If mix shift slows, pricing has to do too much of the work


VCYT

Recent quarter trend
- Q4 2025 revenue growth: +18.5%
- Q4 2025 test-volume growth: +16.2%
- Q4 2025 revenue per test: about $2,988, up +4.2%
- Quarter read: revenues kept accelerating and margin expansion accelerated further
- Main drivers: Decipher growth of 21% and Afirma growth of 12%, though total test-volume growth slowed from Q3

Forward quarter trend
- Q1 2026 revenue growth: +13.8%
- Q2 2026 revenue growth: +13.1%
- Q3 2026 revenue growth: +10.8%
- Q4 2026 revenue growth: +9.1%
- Q1 2026 test-volume growth: +14.5%
- Q2 2026 test-volume growth: +16.6%
- Q3 2026 test-volume growth: +12.8%
- Q4 2026 test-volume growth: +13.6%
- Q1 2026 revenue per test growth: +3.0%
- Q2 2026 revenue per test growth: +0.7%
- Q3 2026 revenue per test growth: -0.8%
- Q4 2026 revenue per test growth: -3.0%
- Forward read: growth stays healthy but decelerates, and the model assumes less help from pricing in the back half

Guidance
- FY26 revenue: $570M-$582M, about +12% versus +16% last year
- Guidance does not include contribution from TrueMRD or Prosigna LDT

Upside drivers
- TrueMRD launch contribution is not in guidance
- Prosigna LDT launch contribution is not in guidance
- Decipher remains a strong growth engine
- If mix stays favorable, revenue per test may hold up better than current estimates assume

Risks
- Prosigna LDT launch could create execution or margin headwinds
- TrueMRD still needs to convert submission into real reimbursement and contribution
- Afirma is decelerating toward mid-single-digit growth
- MRD is a more competitive market, so TrueMRD may face tougher adoption and pricing dynamics
- If mix weakens more than expected, back-half pricing could disappoint further
